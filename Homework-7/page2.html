My Short Story



I'd like to take the time to explain the significance of this picture and what my brother means to me.

We were in San Francisco with our friend Josh. Josh showed me some
new camera techniques and I was taking photos of my brother with the bay in the background.

My brother is one of my inspirations to create artwork. He is always there
encouraging me and giving me constructive feedback. He also used to coach
me on using Adobe Premiere and I learned a lot from him.

I really like this photo that Josh took of me and my brother. I think
it says a lot. I edited this in Photoshop to give it a sketch effect and added my logo.
